    CHAPTER FIVE: SAMOLINA

    An unforeseen entry nudged her way into the canine family of 79 Tilla in 2022. One morning, my grandmother called me. “Look at this!” She said. Socrates was sleeping on the landing. Beside him was a little black bundle that looked like a cat. Wait, a cat? A cat sleeping with a big dog? I looked closely. It was a tiny little bag of bones puppy. It had large, limpid, fearful eyes. It’s tail was straight. It had a small face and two large ears. It was a she. I named her Samolina. Sam was great friends with Socrates. The big dog would not even look at Sam’s food at that time. Now he tries to rob every dog of their dinners. I gave her some biscuits. When she sat up, I noticed that her hind legs were trembling. Her previous master had hit her, maybe because she was not good-looking, with her straight tail. But her owner had turned her out and hit her, all for her appearance. How can people commit such vindictive acts? Anyway, I only wanted to make Samolina comfortable. Goutam Uncle informed me that he had gone to inspect a construction site. Socrates went with him. At the site, they had found scared little Sam. Socrates had brought her to 79 Tilla. She was very afraid in front of humans. In the afternoon, she was play-fighting with Nonsense. At night, she would not move from the landing to eat her foodstuff. Nonsense came, clearly wanting to show that she was the elder sister. Non lugged the whining Sam down the stairs. But Samolina crept into the culvert and hid in the dank weeds, occasionally peeking over the stone partition. We laid out food for her by the rosebush. She eventually came out and gorged up the food.

    Samolina is cheerful and in the pink now. She is glossy black with a white streak. Dabbhai says that a dog he knows, Gian, looks exactly like Samolina. But Gian is fierce and passionate while Sam is as calm and sweeet as cotton candy clouds. She eats as well as Nonsense and Sweetie. She likes to nudge me and lick me. And when I pet her, her face looks contented and her eyes are half-closed. Her face looks really cute and cuddly. Except when she is in her play fights with Nonsense or fighting over a chew toy. When she bares her teeth she looks a miniature Madame Hyena. Socrates loves her. Nonsense maybe thinks her to be Chocolate II. Sweetiepie tries to ignore her, as she is not her daughter, but they are often caught playing or sitting together. I-am-so-adorable-I-can-get-away-with-anything is her attitude now. And she definitely does! Her large, brown, dreamy eyes are just like warm Dairy Milk chocolates. Those eyes just about melt the hearts of everybody, whether a heart of ice or heart of butter. So Didia, Dabbhai, Omdada and I ignore every peskiness Sam ever did and just pet her. She seems to smooth cuteness about her like strawberry jam. Just think how fate acted with Sam! First an emaciated scared little puppy, then an active and happy three-month-old pup!
